About Me

I recently completed my M.S in Cognitive Science at Yonsei University working with Sang Chul Chong in Vision Cognition and Consciousness Laboratory. Prior to that I have been a member of the Cognitive Engineering Square working with KwangSu Cho.

I have been going back and forth the disciplines of cognitive science and HCI, using methods such as psychophysics, eye-tracking, EEG, and survey. ….. The bilateral pursuit… led me to deeper inquiry into human vision… My future goal is to use computational approach to build predictive models of human attention and also to incorporate the models into design applications.
